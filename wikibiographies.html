Biography Article Tips

In this activity you will adopt three tips for writing a good bio article. You should advocate these tips throught the editing session! Tips are from “Ten Simple Rules for Women in Red” by WikiProject Women in Red. Have fun!

1. Choose articles carefully

2. Do your research

3. Write why they are important

4. Cite everything

5. Write in your own words

6. Wikilinks

7. Layout

8. Further additions

9. Talk page

10. Images
